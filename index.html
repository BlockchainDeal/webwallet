FLASH is a new kind of online network that connects people with websites they enjoy and products they love by using the latest crypto coin technology.

FLASH members earn rewards when they participate with publishers, merchants, and advertisers. It’s easy and free to join the FLASH network by getting a FLASH wallet.

Members earn FLASHcoins by participating with online partners. Advertisers and merchants who would like to get larger volumes of FLASHcoins for their projects can purchase them on an exchange, or directly from the FLASHcoin.io web site.

FAQ

We created 900 billion FLASHcoins

Why did we make so many?

FLASHcoins are for everybody, all 7 billion of us.

We wanted Flash to have a number of characteristics:

  • 1. Give the FLASHcoins to users and developers, not miners (since we’re pre-mined).

  • 2. FLASHcoins can circulate widely because they are easy to get in small amounts

  • 3. They will become valuable because they are useful in large amounts for advertising, marketing and other utilities. You buy a large amount through the exchange, which creates a real sustainable model.

  • 4. FLASHcoins will be mostly distributed within about two years, mainly to the users and developers. Help grow the network and earn more FLASHcoins.

  • 5. The FLASHcoin supply is limited. When they are gone, transaction fees will apply (just like bitcoin). Right now we’re saying the FLASHcoins have no value, but that could change in the future.

  • WHY SHOULD I BUY FLASHCOINS IN THE CROWDSALE?

    This is the best way to support our project so FLASH does not have to take outside money with a lot of strings attached. When the community supports FLASH, everyone benefits. Large volumes of coins for your project or just to keep for a rainy day can be found in the crowdsale and eventually on the Bittrex exchange.

  • CAN I DO ANY MINING? HOW CAN I EARN FREE COINS?

    FLASH does the mining for you in a more efficient and fast way. This saves a lot of electricity, and we think it’s better for the environment. Our Whitepaper goes through some of the technical details. We decided to reward people who build the FLASH network, the developers and users. There are a lot of ways to earn rewards using FLASH, such as inviting new users, creating an application that uses FLASH, etc.

  • DO I HAVE TO STORE MY COINS IN THE FLASH ONLINE WALLET?

    Yes. This is a permissioned private blockchain and storing your coins in the FLASH wallet makes it easier for beginning users. We don’t actually store your key, we regenerate it from your password when you login. You also have a way to recover your password. We think these are key features to make crypto more user friendly.

  • WHAT CAN I DO WITH FLASH?

    Beyond sending and receiving FLASHcoins, you can develop applications, participate in rewarding activities and make donations. The possible uses for FLASH are only limited by our members’ creativity.
